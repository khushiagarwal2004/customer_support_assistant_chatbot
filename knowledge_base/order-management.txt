
ORDER MANAGEMENT
================

PLACING AN ORDER
  Add items to cart
  Click Checkout
  Enter or select delivery address
  Select payment method
  Review order summary
  Click Place Order
  Order confirmation email sent within 5 minutes
  SMS confirmation sent to registered mobile number
  Order ID format: ORD-YYYYMMDD-XXXXXX
  Example order ID: ORD-20241215-884521
  Guest checkout available — no account required
  Guest orders tracked via email link only

ORDER STATUS EXPLANATIONS
  Order Placed:
    Payment confirmed, order entered into system
    Warehouse notified to begin processing

  Processing:
    Warehouse team picking and packing your items
    Cannot be cancelled once processing begins

  Ready to Ship:
    Order packed and awaiting carrier pickup
    Tracking ID assigned but not yet active

  Shipped:
    Package handed to carrier
    Tracking ID active — use it to track delivery
    Email and SMS with tracking link sent

  Out for Delivery:
    Package with local delivery agent today
    Delivery expected by 8 PM
    You may receive a call from delivery agent

  Delivered:
    Package marked as delivered by carrier
    Photo proof of delivery saved
    If not received — report within 48 hours

  Return Initiated:
    Return request submitted and approved
    Awaiting pickup from your address

  Return Picked Up:
    Item collected from your address
    Quality check in progress at warehouse

  Refund Initiated:
    Quality check passed
    Refund processing to original payment method

  Cancelled:
    Order cancelled successfully
    Refund initiated for prepaid orders

CANCELLATION POLICY

  Before dispatch (Order Placed or Processing status):
    Cancel from My Orders > Select Order > Cancel Order
    No cancellation fee
    Refund processed immediately for prepaid orders
    COD orders cancelled at no charge

  After dispatch (Shipped or Out for Delivery status):
    Cancellation not possible once shipped
    Option 1: Refuse delivery when it arrives
              Prepaid: refund initiated after QC
              COD: simply refuse — no charge
    Option 2: Accept delivery then raise return request

  Partial cancellation:
    Cancel individual items from multi-item order
    Only possible before dispatch of that item
    Remaining items ship as normal
    Partial refund for cancelled items processed immediately

  Cancellation by ShopEasy:
    We may cancel orders in these situations:
    - Item goes out of stock after order placed
    - Seller unable to fulfil within 3 business days
    - Suspected fraudulent transaction
    - Delivery not possible to your PIN code
    Full refund issued immediately if we cancel your order
    Rs. 50 store credit compensation given to customer

ADDRESS CHANGE POLICY

  Before dispatch:
    Go to My Orders > Select Order > Edit Delivery Address
    Change to any address in our serviceable network
    No charge for address change before dispatch

  After dispatch:
    Contact support within 12 hours of dispatch notification
    Carrier redirect possible in limited cases
    Additional charge may apply for address redirect: Rs. 50 to Rs. 150
    Not guaranteed — depends on carrier and location
    Not possible for same day deliveries
    Not possible if package is already out for delivery

ORDER MODIFICATIONS
  Quantity changes: Only possible before Processing status begins
  Adding items to existing order: Not possible
  Place a new separate order for additional items
  Removing items: Treated as partial cancellation
  Coupon code: Cannot be added or changed after order is placed
  Payment method: Cannot be changed after order is placed

PRE-ORDERS
  Purchase a product before its official release date
  Full payment collected at time of order
  Order confirmed immediately
  Item ships as soon as it becomes available
  Estimated dispatch date shown on product page and order confirmation
  Cancel pre-order anytime before dispatch — full refund
  Price guarantee: if price drops before dispatch we refund the difference

BACK-ORDERS
  Place order for temporarily out of stock item
  Estimated restock date shown at checkout
  Payment collected at time of order
  Email notification sent when item is dispatched
  Cancel back-order anytime before dispatch — full refund
  If item cannot be restocked within 30 days: automatic cancellation and refund

GIFT ORDERS
  Gift wrapping available at checkout: Rs. 49 per item
  Gift message included: up to 150 characters
  Invoice suppressed inside package for gift orders
  Digital invoice sent to buyer email only
  Gift receipt included in package for recipient
  Recipient can use gift receipt for returns within standard window

BULK ORDERS
  Orders above Rs. 50,000 or 20+ units of same SKU

  Special pricing available for bulk buyers
  Dedicated account manager assigned
  Custom delivery scheduling available
  Payment terms: advance payment or verified credit terms

BUSINESS ORDERS
  GST invoice with your GSTIN for all business purchases

  Net 30 payment terms for verified business accounts

ORDER HISTORY
  All orders accessible: My Account > Orders
  Filter by: status, date range, category, price
  Re-order button available for all previously purchased items
  Download invoice from order detail page
  Download warranty card from order detail page
  Order history retained for 5 years

MISSING ITEM IN ORDER
  Report within 48 hours of delivery
  Go to My Orders > Report Issue > Missing Item
  Photo of package and received items required
  Resolution within 24 to 48 hours:
    Option 1: Missing item dispatched separately
    Option 2: Refund for missing item value

WRONG ITEM DELIVERED
  Report within 48 hours of delivery
  Go to My Orders > Report Issue > Wrong Item
  Photo of received item and packaging required
  Do not use the wrong item
  Pickup scheduled within 48 hours
  Correct item dispatched simultaneously with pickup

ORDER NOT RECEIVED — SHOWS DELIVERED
  Check with family, neighbours, building security first
  Check door, mailbox, reception area
  Report within 48 hours: My Orders > Report Issue > Not Received
  We investigate with carrier
  Resolution within 24 to 48 hours
  Full replacement or refund guaranteed
