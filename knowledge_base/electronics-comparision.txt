Which TV size is right for you:
- 32 inch: Bedroom or small room, viewing distance 4-6 feet
- 43 inch: Medium living room, viewing distance 6-9 feet
- 55 inch: Large living room, viewing distance 9-12 feet

Which TV technology is right for you:
- LED/LCD (budget): Best brightness, no burn-in risk, good for bright rooms
- QLED (mid-premium): Better colours than LED, excellent for daytime viewing
- OLED (premium): Perfect blacks, infinite contrast, best for movies and gaming

COMMON CUSTOMER QUESTIONS FOR COMPARISONS
==========================================

Q: Should I buy iPhone or Android?
A: Buy iPhone if you already use MacBook, iPad, or AirPods — the ecosystem integration is seamless.
   Buy Android (Samsung or Pixel) if you want more customisation, better value, or Google services.

Q: Is OLED worth the premium over QLED?
A: For movie watching in a dark room — yes, OLED is significantly better.
   For bright rooms or gaming — QLED is often better due to higher brightness.
   OLED has a small risk of burn-in with static content displayed for long periods.

Q: Should I buy now or wait for sale?
A: Electronics prices drop most significantly during:
   - Republic Day Sale (January)
   - Independence Day Sale (August)
   - Festive Season Sale (October-November — biggest discounts)
   If you need the product urgently buy now — waiting is only worthwhile near these periods.

Q: Which smartphone has the best camera for the price?
A: Under Rs. 15,000: Redmi Note 13 (108MP)
   Under Rs. 25,000: Nothing Phone 2a (dual 50MP system)
   Under Rs. 30,000: Samsung Galaxy A55 (triple camera with OIS)
   Premium: Google Pixel 9 (best computational photography regardless of price)

Q: What is the difference between 4G and 5G phones?
A: 5G phones can use faster 5G networks where available.
   In most Indian cities 5G coverage is now available from Jio and Airtel.
   5G phones cost slightly more but future-proof your purchase for 3-4 years.
   If you plan to keep the phone for 3+ years, buy 5G.
   If budget is tight and your area has good 4G, 4G is still perfectly fine.

Q: What warranty do electronics come with?
A: All smartphones: 1 year manufacturer warranty
   Laptops: 1 year manufacturer warranty (extended available at checkout)
   Televisions: 1 year standard, LG OLED 5-year panel warranty on select models
   Extended warranty is strongly recommended for laptops and TVs — available at checkout
