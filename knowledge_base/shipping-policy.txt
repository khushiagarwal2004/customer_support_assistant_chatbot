SHIPPING POLICY
===============

SERVICE COVERAGE
We deliver to 27,000+ pin codes across all 28 Indian states
and 8 Union Territories. Coverage includes all Tier-1, Tier-2,
and most Tier-3 cities and towns.
International shipping: Not currently available.
Remote and difficult terrain areas: Additional 2-3 days added
to standard delivery timelines.

DELIVERY OPTIONS & TIMELINES

Standard Delivery:
  Metro cities:
    Mumbai, Delhi, Bengaluru, Chennai, Kolkata, Hyderabad,
    Pune, Ahmedabad — 3 to 5 business days
  Tier-2 cities:
    Jaipur, Lucknow, Nagpur, Indore, Coimbatore, Surat,
    Vadodara, Patna, Bhopal, Visakhapatnam — 4 to 6 business days
  Tier-3 and semi-urban areas:
    6 to 9 business days
  Remote PIN codes:
    8 to 12 business days

Express Delivery (Next Business Day):
  Available in 22 cities across India
  Order must be placed before 12 PM for next day delivery
  Carrier: Bluedart Premium
  Charge: Rs. 99 flat
  Charge waived for:
    - Orders above Rs. 2,999
    - All Prime membership orders
  Not available for items above 10kg or oversized packages

Same Day Delivery:
  Available cities: Mumbai, Delhi NCR, Bengaluru, Pune, Hyderabad
  Orders must be placed before 10 AM
  Delivered by 8 PM the same day
  Charge: Rs. 149
  Charge waived for Prime members on orders above Rs. 499
  Not available for large or heavy items above 10kg
  Live GPS tracking link shared 30 minutes before delivery

Scheduled Delivery:
  Choose your preferred delivery date up to 7 days in advance
  Choose AM slot: 9 AM to 1 PM
  Choose PM slot: 2 PM to 8 PM
  Available for all standard delivery zones
  Free of charge — no additional cost

FREE SHIPPING CONDITIONS
  All orders above Rs. 499 — standard delivery free
  All Prime membership orders — free regardless of order value
  All Plus membership orders above Rs. 299 — free standard delivery
  Items tagged "Free Ship" — free regardless of order value

SHIPPING CHARGES FOR ORDERS BELOW Rs. 499
  Standard delivery:  Rs. 40
  Express delivery:   Rs. 99
  Same day delivery:  Rs. 149

CARRIERS WE USE
  Delhivery:       Standard delivery, widest pin code coverage
  Bluedart:        Express and same day deliveries
  DTDC:            Remote and difficult terrain areas
  Xpressbees:      Tier-2 and Tier-3 cities
  Amazon Logistics: Select pin codes
  Ecom Express:    Backup carrier for high demand periods

ORDER TRACKING
  Tracking ID shared via email and SMS within 24 hours of dispatch
  Track your order: My Account > My Orders > Track Shipment
  WhatsApp tracking updates available — opt in from account settings
  Live GPS tracking available for same day deliveries only
  If tracking link not received within 24 hours of dispatch
  confirmation — contact support

DELIVERY ATTEMPTS
  Carrier attempts delivery twice before returning to hub
  After 2 failed attempts: package held at nearest hub for 5 days
  Reschedule delivery from the tracking link in your email or SMS
  After 5 days at hub with no response: package returned to warehouse
  Prepaid orders: automatic refund initiated after return to warehouse
  COD orders: no charge if delivery fails

LARGE ITEMS & FURNITURE DELIVERY
  Scheduled delivery only — 3 to 14 business days
  Delivery team calls 24 hours before scheduled delivery
  Ground floor delivery included in standard charges
  Above ground floor: Rs. 200 per flight of stairs
  Installation service available for select categories
  Installation charged separately — shown at checkout
  Delivery team will not install without booking installation service

PACKAGING STANDARDS
  Eco-friendly packaging used wherever possible
  Fragile items: double-boxed with bubble wrap protection
  Electronics: antistatic packaging with tamper-evident seals
  All packages sealed with ShopEasy branded tape
  Liquid items: leak-proof secondary packaging
  Tamper-evident seal — do not accept if seal is broken

WHAT TO DO IF PACKAGE IS DAMAGED ON ARRIVAL
  Do not accept a visibly damaged package from the delivery agent
  If damage noticed after opening: photograph immediately
  Report within 48 hours via My Orders > Report Issue
  Do not dispose of packaging — keep it for pickup
  Replacement dispatched within 24 to 48 hours of report

WHAT TO DO IF PACKAGE SHOWS DELIVERED BUT NOT RECEIVED
  First check with family members, neighbours, building security
  Check if item was left at door or reception
  If still not found: report within 48 hours via My Orders > Report Issue
  We investigate with carrier and resolve within 24 to 48 hours
  Full replacement or refund guaranteed for undelivered orders

SHIPPING RESTRICTIONS
  Certain items cannot be shipped to all locations:
  - Flammable or hazardous items: restricted pin codes only
  - High-value items above Rs. 50,000: signature required on delivery
  - Alcohol and tobacco: only to states where legally permitted
  - Oversized items: metro cities and select Tier-2 cities only
