
PAYMENT POLICY
==============

ACCEPTED PAYMENT METHODS

Credit Cards:
  Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Diners Club, RuPay
  International credit cards accepted
  3D Secure authentication required for all transactions
  Saved cards stored securely via tokenization

Debit Cards:
  All major bank debit cards accepted
  Supported networks: Visa, Mastercard, Maestro, RuPay
  Banks: SBI, HDFC, ICICI, Axis, Kotak, Yes Bank, PNB,
         Bank of Baroda, Canara Bank, and 50+ more

UPI (Unified Payments Interface):
  Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM, Amazon Pay, Cred Pay
  Pay via UPI ID or scan QR code at checkout
  Instant payment confirmation
  No additional charges for UPI payments

Net Banking:
  60+ banks supported including all major nationalized banks
  Redirected to your bank portal for secure authentication
  Payment confirmed instantly on return to ShopEasy

Digital Wallets:
  Paytm Wallet, Amazon Pay balance, Mobikwik, Freecharge
  Wallet balance can be partially used with another payment method
  Example: Rs. 200 from wallet + remaining via UPI

EMI (Equated Monthly Installments):
  Available on all orders above Rs. 3,000
  No-cost EMI available on select products
    (look for "No Cost EMI" tag on product page)
  Supported banks: SBI, HDFC, ICICI, Axis, Kotak, Yes Bank,
                   Citibank, HSBC, Standard Chartered
  Available tenures: 3, 6, 9, 12, 18, 24 months
  Processing fee: Nil on no-cost EMI
  Processing fee on standard EMI: 1% to 2% of order value
  Debit card EMI available for HDFC and Axis bank customers
  EMI eligibility shown automatically at checkout

Buy Now Pay Later (BNPL):
  Available providers: ZestMoney, Simpl, LazyPay, Flipkart Pay Later
  Credit limit: Rs. 500 to Rs. 1,00,000 depending on provider
  Repayment cycle: 15-day or monthly depending on provider
  Eligibility checked automatically at checkout
  No interest for pay-in-full within due date

Cash on Delivery (COD):
  Available on orders up to Rs. 10,000
  Not available in certain remote PIN codes
  Not available on combo offers or heavily discounted items
  COD convenience charge: Rs. 29 per order
  COD charge waived for Prime membership holders
  Exact change appreciated — delivery agent carries limited change
  COD orders can be cancelled before dispatch without any charge

Store Credit & Gift Cards:
  Apply at checkout in the Promotions or Gift Card section
  Partial use allowed — remaining balance saved to your account
  Store credit valid for 1 year from date of issue
  Gift cards valid for 2 years from purchase date
  Store credit and gift cards cannot be converted to cash

PAYMENT SECURITY
  Payment gateway: Razorpay (PCI-DSS Level 1 certified)
  Your actual card number is never stored on our servers
  All card data stored as secure tokens only
  All transactions encrypted via TLS 1.3
  Two-factor authentication for high-value transactions above Rs. 50,000
  Suspicious transaction alerts sent via email and SMS
  If you did not initiate a transaction — contact us immediately

SAVED PAYMENT METHODS
  Save cards, UPI IDs, and wallets for faster checkout
  Tokenization ensures your real card number is never stored
  Manage saved payment methods: My Account > Payment Methods
  Delete saved methods anytime from account settings
  Maximum 5 saved cards per account

PAYMENT FAILURE — CARD DECLINED
  If your payment is declined:
  - Order is not placed
  - No amount is deducted
  Common reasons for decline:
    - Daily transaction limit reached on your card or UPI
    - International card used without enabling online transactions
    - Bank security block on first-time ecommerce transaction
    - Incorrect CVV or expiry date entered
    - Insufficient balance
  Solutions:
    - Try a different payment method
    - Contact your bank to check transaction limits
    - Enable online transactions via your bank app

PAYMENT DEDUCTED BUT ORDER NOT CONFIRMED
  This happens when payment is processed but order confirmation fails.
  Follow these steps:

  Step 1: Wait 30 minutes
          Most auto-reversals complete within this window

  Step 2: Check My Orders
          Order may have confirmed — refresh the page

  Step 3: Check your bank statement
          Confirm the deduction actually occurred

  Step 4: If amount not reversed in 5 to 7 business days:
          Raise a support ticket via My Account > Help

  Step 5: Share these details with support team:
          - Your registered email or mobile number
          - Order date and time
          - Bank transaction UTR number
          - Amount deducted
          - Screenshot of bank statement (optional but helpful)

  Step 6: Finance team investigates within 24 hours
          Full refund guaranteed if order was not placed

DUPLICATE CHARGE
  If charged twice for the same order:
  - Share both transaction IDs with support team
  - Duplicate charge refunded within 48 hours
  - Email: payments@shopeasy.com with subject "Duplicate Charge"

EMI REFUNDS
  If an EMI order is returned:
  - Outstanding EMI balance cancelled
  - Amount already paid refunded to original card
  - Bank processes EMI reversal within 7 to 10 business days
  - Processing fee charged by bank may not be refunded
    (depends on your bank policy)

GST INVOICE & BILLING
  GST invoice generated automatically for all orders
  Download invoice: My Orders > Select Order > Download Invoice
  Invoice emailed to registered email within 24 hours of delivery
  Business buyers: add GSTIN in My Account > Business Details
  B2B invoice with GSTIN issued for business accounts
  Invoice cannot be modified after generation
