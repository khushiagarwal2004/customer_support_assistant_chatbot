
RETURN & REFUND POLICY
======================

STANDARD RETURN WINDOW
Our standard return window is 30 days from the date of delivery.
Items must meet all of the following conditions to be eligible:
- Item must be unused and in original condition
- All original packaging must be intact
- All tags, labels, and stickers must be attached
- Original invoice or order confirmation must be provided
- All accessories, manuals, and free gifts included with the product must be returned

CATEGORY-SPECIFIC RETURN WINDOWS

Electronics & Appliances:
- Return window: 15 days from delivery
- Item must include all original accessories (charger, cables, earphones, remote)
- Original box with serial number must be present
- Serial number on item must match serial number on box and invoice
- No physical damage, scratches, or signs of use accepted
- Software must not be activated or registered

Fashion & Clothing:
- Return window: 30 days from delivery
- Item must be unworn and unwashed
- All tags must be intact and attached
- No perfume, deodorant, or any odour on the item
- No alterations or tailoring done to the item

Footwear:
- Return window: 30 days from delivery
- Shoes must be unworn — soles must show no signs of use
- Original shoebox must be returned
- Both shoes must be returned together

Books, Software & Digital Downloads:
- Non-returnable once delivered or downloaded
- No exceptions unless item is physically damaged on arrival

Innerwear, Swimwear & Personal Care:
- Non-returnable for hygiene reasons
- No exceptions

Perishables (Food, Plants, Flowers):
- Non-returnable
- Contact support within 2 hours if item arrives damaged

Customised & Personalised Items:
- Non-returnable unless item arrives defective or damaged
- Includes monogrammed, engraved, or made-to-order items

Festive Sale Items (marked "Final Sale"):
- Non-returnable unless item arrives damaged or defective
- "Final Sale" tag is shown clearly on product page at time of purchase

Furniture & Large Appliances:
- Return window: 7 days from delivery
- Installation team inspection required before return is accepted
- Item must not have been assembled or installed by customer
- Original packaging preferred but not mandatory

DAMAGED OR DEFECTIVE ITEMS
- Report within 48 hours of delivery — do not delay
- Clear photos of the damage and packaging required
- Eligible resolutions:
    Option 1: Full replacement (subject to stock availability)
    Option 2: Full refund to original payment method
    Option 3: Store credit worth 110% of item value
- Manufacturing defects discovered within 6 months → warranty replacement
- We arrange pickup at no cost for damaged or defective items

RETURN PROCESS — STEP BY STEP
Step 1: Log in to your account
Step 2: Go to My Orders
Step 3: Select the item you want to return
Step 4: Click Return / Exchange
Step 5: Select reason from dropdown:
         - Damaged on arrival
         - Wrong item delivered
         - Changed my mind
         - Size or fit issue
         - Quality not as expected
         - Other
Step 6: Upload photos (mandatory for damage claims)
Step 7: Submit request
Step 8: Pickup scheduled within 48 hours of approval
Step 9: Quality check at warehouse takes 2-3 business days
Step 10: Refund initiated after QC approval

REFUND TIMELINES
Credit / Debit Card:      5-7 business days after QC approval
UPI:                      3-5 business days after QC approval
Net Banking:              5-7 business days after QC approval
COD orders:               7-10 business days via NEFT bank transfer
Store Credit:             Instant — valid for 1 year from issue date
EMI purchases:            Reversed to card, bank processes in 7-10 days
Wallet payments:          3-5 business days back to wallet

NON-REFUNDABLE CHARGES
- Original shipping fee is non-refundable unless the item was defective
- Gift wrapping charges are non-refundable
- Installation charges are refunded only if the product itself is returned

EXCHANGE POLICY
- Direct exchange available for size or colour issues in fashion and footwear
- Subject to stock availability at time of exchange request
- Exchange pickup and new item delivery coordinated simultaneously where possible
- If exchange item costs more: pay the difference at checkout
- If exchange item costs less: difference refunded to original payment method
- Electronics and appliances: exchanges not available — return and repurchase

RETURN REJECTION REASONS
A return may be rejected after quality check if:
- Item shows signs of use or damage not present at delivery
- Original packaging is missing or damaged
- Accessories or tags are missing
- Item does not match what was originally ordered
- Return window has expired
Contact support within 48 hours of rejection to dispute a QC decision.
