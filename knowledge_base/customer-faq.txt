
CUSTOMER FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
=====================================

ORDERS & DELIVERY

Q: How do I track my order?
A: Go to My Account > My Orders and click Track next to your order.
   You will also receive a tracking link via email and SMS within
   24 hours of dispatch. For same day deliveries a live map link
   is shared 30 minutes before the delivery agent arrives.
   If you have not received a tracking link within 24 hours of
   your dispatch confirmation email contact our support team.

Q: Can I order without creating an account?
A: Yes. Guest checkout is available on ShopEasy.
   Enter your email address and delivery address at checkout.
   Order confirmation and tracking updates are sent to your email.
   However without an account you cannot:
   - Use saved addresses or payment methods
   - Earn or redeem ShopCoins reward points
   - Access easy returns from My Orders
   - View full order history
   We recommend creating a free account for a better experience.

Q: My order shows Delivered but I have not received it.
A: First check with family members, neighbours, and building security.
   Check your door, mailbox, and reception area.
   Delivery agents sometimes leave packages with neighbours or security.
   If you still cannot find it report within 48 hours:
   Go to My Orders > Select Order > Report Issue > Not Received.
   We will investigate with the carrier and resolve within 24 to 48 hours.
   Full replacement or refund is guaranteed for undelivered orders.

Q: Can I change my delivery address after placing an order?
A: Before dispatch: Go to My Orders > Select Order > Edit Address.
   You can change to any address in our serviceable network.
   After dispatch: Contact support within 12 hours of dispatch.
   Address changes after dispatch are possible in limited cases
   and may incur a small redirect charge of Rs. 50 to Rs. 150.
   Address changes are not possible for same day deliveries.
   Address changes are not possible if order is already out for delivery.

Q: Why is my order delayed?
A: Delays can happen due to:
   - High demand periods such as sale events
   - Weather disruptions or natural calamities
   - Carrier capacity constraints
   - Address verification issues
   - Remote delivery location
   Check your tracking link for the latest status update.
   If estimated delivery date has passed by more than 2 days
   contact support for a status update or replacement dispatch.

Q: What does Out for Delivery mean and will it arrive today?
A: Out for Delivery means your package is with a local delivery
   agent and is scheduled for delivery today.
   Delivery is typically completed by 8 PM.
   In rare cases due to heavy traffic or large delivery loads
   it may slip to the next day.
   You will receive a notification if delivery is rescheduled.
   You may also receive a call from the delivery agent.

Q: What happens if I miss my delivery?
A: The carrier will attempt delivery twice before returning to hub.
   After two failed attempts the package is held at the nearest
   delivery hub for 5 days.
   You can reschedule delivery from the tracking link in your
   email or SMS notification.
   After 5 days with no response the package is returned to our
   warehouse and an automatic refund is initiated for prepaid orders.

Q: Can I get same day delivery for all products?
A: Same day delivery is available in Mumbai, Delhi NCR, Bengaluru,
   Pune, and Hyderabad only.
   Orders must be placed before 10 AM.
   Items above 10kg or oversized packages are not eligible.
   Same day delivery charge is Rs. 149 (free for Prime members
   on orders above Rs. 499).

RETURNS & REFUNDS

Q: How long does a refund take after I return an item?
A: After your return is picked up it undergoes quality check
   at our warehouse which takes 2 to 3 business days.
   After QC approval refund timelines are:
   Credit or Debit Card: 5 to 7 business days
   UPI: 3 to 5 business days
   Net Banking: 5 to 7 business days
   COD orders via bank transfer: 7 to 10 business days
   Store credit: Instant
   If your refund has not arrived after these timelines
   contact support with your order ID and return ID.

Q: Can I return an electronic item if I simply do not like it?
A: Yes within 15 days if the item is unused and in original
   packaging with all accessories and original box included.
   If the item has been used returns are only accepted for
   manufacturing defects or damage present at delivery.
   Change of mind returns for used electronics are not accepted.
   The serial number on the item must match the invoice.

Q: My return was rejected after quality check. Why?
A: Returns are rejected after QC if:
   - Item shows signs of use or damage not present at delivery
   - Original packaging is missing or severely damaged
   - Accessories tags or manuals are missing
   - Serial number does not match (electronics)
   - Item does not match what was originally ordered
   - Return window had expired at time of request
   If you believe the rejection was incorrect contact support
   within 48 hours of the rejection notification to dispute it.

Q: Can I exchange instead of returning?
A: Direct exchange is available for fashion and footwear only
   for size or colour issues.
   Select Exchange instead of Return when raising the request.
   Choose the correct size or colour you want.
   Exchange pickup and new item delivery are coordinated together.
   If exchange item costs more you pay the difference.
   If exchange item costs less the difference is refunded.
   For electronics and other categories:
   complete a return and then place a new order.

Q: Can I return a product after the return window has expired?
A: Standard returns are not accepted after the return window closes.
   However if the product has a manufacturing defect you may be
   eligible for a warranty claim even after the return window.
   Warranty claims: My Orders > Select Order > Claim Warranty.
   Contact support if you believe your case deserves an exception.
   Prime members have an extended 45-day return window.

PAYMENTS & PRICING

Q: Why was my payment declined even though I have enough balance?
A: Common reasons for payment decline:
   - Daily transaction limit reached on your card or UPI
   - International card used without enabling online transactions
   - Bank security block on first-time ecommerce transaction
   - Incorrect CVV or expiry date entered
   - Card expired
   - Insufficient balance including pending charges
   Solutions:
   Try a different payment method.
   Contact your bank to check and increase transaction limits.
   Enable online or ecommerce transactions via your bank app.

Q: Is it safe to save my card details on ShopEasy?
A: Yes. We use Razorpay as our payment gateway which is
   PCI-DSS Level 1 certified — the highest level of payment security.
   Your actual card number is never stored on our servers.
   Only a secure token is saved — the actual number cannot be
   retrieved from the token even if our systems were compromised.
   You can delete saved cards anytime from My Account > Payment Methods.

Q: Can I use multiple payment methods for one order?
A: You can combine Store Credit or ShopCoins with any other
   payment method for the remaining amount.
   Two credit cards or two UPI accounts cannot be combined.
   Wallet balance can be combined with a card for the remaining amount.
   Example: Rs. 500 store credit + Rs. 1200 via UPI for a Rs. 1700 order.

Q: The price changed between adding to cart and checkout. Why?
A: Prices on ShopEasy are dynamic and can change due to:
   - Flash sale ending between cart and checkout
   - Promotional period expiring
   - Seller updating their price
   - Stock level changes affecting pricing
   Adding an item to your cart does not lock or reserve the price.
   The price shown at checkout is the final price.
   We recommend completing checkout promptly during sales.

Q: Are there any hidden charges on ShopEasy?
A: No hidden charges. The total shown at checkout is what you pay.
   Charges that may be added at checkout:
   - Delivery charge if order is below free shipping threshold
   - COD convenience fee of Rs. 29 (waived for Prime members)
   - Gift wrapping charge of Rs. 49 per item if selected
   - Installation charge if booked for applicable products
   All charges are shown clearly before you place the order.
   GST is included in the product price shown — not added separately.

Q: What is No Cost EMI?
A: No Cost EMI means you pay exactly the product price split
   across monthly installments with no additional interest.
   The bank interest is absorbed by ShopEasy or the seller.
   Available on select products marked with No Cost EMI tag.
   Available on orders above Rs. 3,000.
   Supported banks include SBI, HDFC, ICICI, Axis, Kotak, Yes Bank.
   Tenures available: 3, 6, 9, 12, 18, 24 months.
   Standard EMI is also available and includes processing fee
   of 1% to 2% of the order value.

ACCOUNT & MEMBERSHIP

Q: I forgot my password. How do I reset it?
A: Click Forgot Password on the login page.
   Enter your registered email address or mobile number.
   You will receive an OTP to verify your identity.
   Enter the OTP and set a new password.
   Password must be minimum 8 characters with uppercase,
   lowercase, number, and special character.
   If you have lost access to both your email and mobile
   contact support with government-issued ID proof for
   manual account verification.

Q: How do I cancel my Prime membership?
A: Go to My Account > Prime Membership > Manage > Cancel Renewal.
   Your Prime benefits remain active until the end of your
   current billing period.
   No partial refunds are given for unused membership period.
   You can reactivate Prime at any time after cancellation.

Q: What are ShopCoins and how do I use them?
A: ShopCoins are ShopEasy reward points.
   You earn 1 ShopCoin for every Rs. 100 spent.
   Prime members earn 2 ShopCoins per Rs. 100 spent.
   1 ShopCoin equals Re. 1 discount at checkout.
   Redeem at checkout under Rewards and Coupons section.
   Maximum 20% of order value can be paid via ShopCoins.
   ShopCoins expire 1 year after the date they were earned.
   Check your balance: My Account > ShopCoins.

Q: Can I merge two ShopEasy accounts?
A: Account merging is not currently supported.
   If you accidentally created two accounts contact support.
   Order history cannot be transferred between accounts.
   ShopCoins consolidation may be possible in some cases
   — contact support to check eligibility.

PRODUCTS & AVAILABILITY

Q: How do I know if a product is genuine?
A: Look for the Authenticity Guaranteed badge on the product page.
   Electronics come only from authorized distributors.
   Branded fashion and footwear come from brand-authorized sellers.
   If you receive a counterfeit item you get a full refund
   plus Rs. 500 store credit as compensation.
   Report suspected counterfeit: trust@shopeasy.com.

Q: The product I want is out of stock. When will it be back?
A: Click Notify Me on the product page to receive an alert
   when it comes back in stock.
   For high-demand items we restock regularly.
   You can also check if the same product is available from
   a different seller on the same listing.
   Back-order option available for select products —
   place order now and receive when stock is replenished.

Q: Why do prices vary for the same product across different sellers?
A: Different sellers set their own prices independently.
   The lowest-priced seller is typically shown first.
   Check seller ratings before choosing a lower-priced option.
   Price differences can be due to:
   - Different seller cost structures
   - Seller promotions or offers
   - New versus open-box condition
   - FBS versus self-fulfilled (affects speed not quality)
   ShopEasy does not set seller prices but monitors for
   price gouging and takes action against violations.

Q: Can I get a product review from a verified buyer?
A: Yes. Only verified purchasers can write reviews on ShopEasy.
   Look for the Verified Purchase badge on reviews.
   Reviews are moderated before publishing (24 to 48 hours).
   You can filter reviews by star rating and recency.
   Write your own review: My Orders > Delivered > Write Review.
   Earn 50 ShopCoins for writing a verified product review.

TECHNICAL & APP

Q: The ShopEasy website or app is not loading. What should I do?
A: Try these steps in order:
   Step 1: Check your internet connection
   Step 2: Clear browser cache and cookies
   Step 3: Try a different browser or device
   Step 4: Update the ShopEasy app to the latest version
   Step 5: Restart your device
   If the issue persists contact support with your device model
   and operating system version.
   Check shopeasy.com/status for any known outages.

Q: How do I download my GST invoice?
A: Go to My Orders.
   Select the delivered order.
   Click Download Invoice.
   GST invoice is auto-generated for all orders.
   Business buyers with GSTIN registered in account settings
   receive B2B invoices automatically.
   Invoices are also emailed to your registered email address
   within 24 hours of delivery confirmation.

Q: How do I write a product review?
A: Only verified purchasers can write reviews.
   Go to My Orders > Select delivered order > Write Review.
   Rate 1 to 5 stars and write your review text.
   Add photos to make your review more helpful.
   Reviews are moderated and published within 24 to 48 hours.
   You earn 50 ShopCoins for each verified review published.
   Reviews cannot be edited after 24 hours of submission.
