
ECOMMERCE GLOSSARY
==================

This glossary explains common ecommerce terms that customers
frequently ask about when shopping on ShopEasy.

SKU (Stock Keeping Unit):
  A unique identifier code assigned to each distinct product variant.
  Example: a blue shirt in size M has a different SKU from the same
  shirt in size L or in a different colour.
  SKUs are used internally to track inventory levels.
  When contacting support about a specific product variant
  the SKU helps us identify it precisely.

MRP (Maximum Retail Price):
  The highest legal price at which a product can be sold in India
  as printed on the product packaging by the manufacturer.
  Our prices are always at or below MRP.
  The percentage off shown on ShopEasy is the discount from MRP.
  MRP includes all taxes — no additional tax is added at checkout.
  Selling above MRP is illegal under Indian consumer protection law.

Fulfilled by ShopEasy (FBS):
  Products stored in our warehouse and shipped by us even though
  sold by a third-party seller.
  FBS orders benefit from:
  - Guaranteed 24-hour dispatch
  - ShopEasy packaging standards
  - Faster delivery timelines
  - Easier returns handled directly by ShopEasy
  Look for the Fulfilled by ShopEasy badge on product listings.

Flash Sale:
  A limited-time sale offering deep discounts typically lasting
  4 to 24 hours with limited stock quantities.
  Items sell out quickly — add to cart immediately.
  Prime members get 12-hour early access to all flash sales.
  Flash sale prices cannot be price-matched after the sale ends.
  Flash sale items may have different return policies
  — check product page for specific terms.

COD (Cash on Delivery):
  Payment made in cash to the delivery agent when the package arrives.
  Available on orders up to Rs. 10,000 on ShopEasy.
  COD convenience charge: Rs. 29 per order (waived for Prime members).
  Exact change is appreciated as delivery agents carry limited change.
  COD orders can be refused at the door if you change your mind
  — no charge for COD refusal.
  Not available in certain remote PIN codes.
  Not available on heavily discounted combo offers.

EMI (Equated Monthly Installment):
  Splitting the total cost of a purchase into equal monthly payments.
  No-cost EMI: you pay exactly the product price split into installments.
  The bank interest is absorbed by ShopEasy or the seller.
  Standard EMI: includes processing fee of 1% to 2% of order value.
  EMI available on orders above Rs. 3,000.
  Supported banks: SBI, HDFC, ICICI, Axis, Kotak, Yes Bank and more.
  Tenures: 3, 6, 9, 12, 18, and 24 months.
  If you return an EMI order outstanding installments are cancelled
  and amount already paid is refunded to your card.

BNPL (Buy Now Pay Later):
  Purchase now and pay within a specified period with no immediate payment.
  Repayment period: typically 15 to 30 days depending on provider.
  Providers on ShopEasy: ZestMoney, Simpl, LazyPay, Flipkart Pay Later.
  Credit limit: Rs. 500 to Rs. 1,00,000 depending on provider and
  your credit score.
  No interest if paid in full by due date.
  Late payment fees apply if due date is missed.
  BNPL eligibility checked automatically at checkout.

Wishlist:
  A saved list of products you are interested in but have not
  purchased yet.
  Wishlisted items trigger automatic price drop notifications
  so you never miss a deal.
  Also triggers stock alerts when out-of-stock items are available.
  Share your wishlist as a gift guide via link.
  Move items from wishlist to cart in one click.
  Items in wishlist do not reserve stock.
  Access wishlist: My Account > Wishlist.

ShopCoins:
  ShopEasy reward points earned on every purchase.
  Standard members earn 1 ShopCoin per Rs. 100 spent.
  Prime members earn 2 ShopCoins per Rs. 100 spent.
  1 ShopCoin equals Re. 1 discount at checkout.
  Maximum 20% of order value can be paid via ShopCoins.
  Minimum redemption: 100 ShopCoins.
  ShopCoins expire 1 year after they are earned.
  Cannot be transferred converted to cash or used on delivery charges.
  Check balance and expiry: My Account > ShopCoins.

RTO (Return to Origin):
  When a delivery fails and the package returns to our warehouse.
  Common causes: two failed delivery attempts, customer refused delivery,
  address not found, customer unavailable repeatedly.
  Prepaid orders: automatic refund initiated after RTO confirmed.
  COD orders: no charge to customer for RTO.
  You will receive email notification when RTO is processed.
  After RTO you can place a fresh order with a corrected address.

PIN Code Serviceability:
  Whether our delivery network covers your postal area.
  Check at checkout by entering your PIN code.
  Most of India's 27,000+ PIN codes are serviceable.
  Remote PIN codes may have longer delivery timelines.
  Some PIN codes not eligible for express or same day delivery.
  Some PIN codes not eligible for COD.
  If your PIN code is not serviceable we will notify you at checkout.

Pre-order:
  Purchasing a product before its official release or availability date.
  Full payment collected at time of order placement.
  Order is confirmed immediately with an estimated dispatch date.
  Item ships as soon as it becomes available.
  Cancel pre-order anytime before dispatch for full refund.
  Price guarantee: if price drops before dispatch we refund the difference.

Back-order:
  Placing an order for an item that is temporarily out of stock.
  Estimated restock date shown at checkout.
  Payment collected at time of order.
  Email notification sent when item is dispatched.
  Cancel back-order anytime before dispatch for full refund.
  If item cannot be restocked within 30 days automatic cancellation
  and full refund are processed.

Quality Check (QC):
  Inspection of returned items at our warehouse before approving refund.
  QC verifies: item condition, completeness of accessories,
  original packaging, serial numbers for electronics.
  QC typically takes 2 to 3 business days after return pickup.
  QC passed: refund initiated within 24 hours.
  QC failed: item returned to customer with explanation.
  Dispute QC decision within 48 hours by contacting support.

UTR (Unique Transaction Reference):
  A reference number assigned by the bank to every transaction.
  Format varies by bank: typically 12 to 22 characters.
  Essential for tracing a payment if order was not confirmed
  but amount was deducted from your account.
  Find UTR in your bank app under transaction history.
  Share UTR with ShopEasy support for payment issue resolution.
  Without UTR payment investigation can take significantly longer.

GST Invoice:
  Tax invoice issued for all purchases on ShopEasy.
  Shows CGST and SGST breakdown for intra-state purchases.
  Shows IGST for inter-state purchases.
  Required for business expense claims and input tax credit.
  Download: My Orders > Select Order > Download Invoice.
  Emailed to registered email within 24 hours of delivery.
  Business buyers add GSTIN in account settings for B2B invoice.
  Invoice cannot be modified after generation.

Tokenization:
  Security process where your actual card number is replaced
  with a random token for storage purposes.
  We never store your real card number — only the token.
  The token is useless to hackers — cannot be used elsewhere.
  Mandated by RBI for all Indian payment processors since 2022.
  Enables one-click checkout without compromising card security.
  You can delete tokens anytime from My Account > Payment Methods.

OTP (One Time Password):
  A temporary numeric code sent to your mobile or email
  to verify your identity for secure actions.
  Valid for 10 minutes only.
  Used for: account login, password reset, high-value transactions,
  address changes, payment verification.
  Never share your OTP with anyone including ShopEasy staff.
  ShopEasy will never ask for your OTP over phone or chat.

Prime Membership:
  ShopEasy's premium subscription at Rs. 999 per year or Rs. 149 per month.
  Benefits include: free unlimited delivery, early sale access,
  extended 45-day returns, priority support, 2x ShopCoins,
  free COD, free gift wrapping, and ShopEasy Prime Video access.
  Family sharing available for up to 2 additional members.
  Cancel anytime — benefits active till end of billing period.

Seller Rating:
  A score from 1.0 to 5.0 reflecting seller performance.
  Based on: delivery speed, packaging quality, product accuracy,
  and customer service responsiveness.
  Visible on all seller product listings.
  Sellers below 4.0 rating face suspension.
  Helps you choose reliable sellers when multiple sellers
  offer the same product at different prices.

Open Box:
  A product that has been opened previously but not used.
  May be a customer return that passed quality check.
  Priced lower than brand new sealed products.
  Clearly labelled as Open Box on listing.
  Comes with same warranty as new product.
  Same return policy as new products applies.
  Condition verified and certified by ShopEasy.

Combo Offer:
  A discounted bundle of two or more related products sold together.
  Combo price is lower than buying items individually.
  All items in a combo must be returned together for full refund.
  Partial returns from combos result in adjusted refund amount.
  COD not available on certain combo offers.
  Combo offers cannot be split into individual items at checkout.

Coupon Code:
  An alphanumeric code that applies a discount at checkout.
  Enter in the Promotions or Coupon Code field at checkout.
  One coupon code per order maximum.
  Cannot be combined with certain other offers.
  Cannot be added after order is placed.
  Coupon codes have expiry dates — check validity before use.
  Find available coupons: My Account > My Coupons.

Delivery Slot:
  A specific time window chosen by the customer for delivery.
  Available for scheduled delivery option only.
  Slots: AM (9 AM to 1 PM) or PM (2 PM to 8 PM).
  Book up to 7 days in advance.
  No additional charge for scheduled delivery slots.
  Slot booking available at checkout or from My Orders
  before the order is dispatched.
