
SELLER & VENDOR POLICY
======================

THIRD-PARTY SELLERS ON SHOPEASY
  ShopEasy is a marketplace platform hosting products from
  verified third-party sellers alongside our own inventory.
  Every product page clearly shows:
    - Sold by: [Seller Name]
    - Fulfilled by: ShopEasy or Seller
    - Seller rating and number of reviews
    - Seller response time

SELLER VERIFICATION & ONBOARDING
  All sellers must complete:
    - GSTIN verification (mandatory for all Indian sellers)
    - PAN card verification
    - Bank account verification
    - Business address verification
    - KYC (Know Your Customer) documentation
  New sellers undergo 30-day probationary period
  During probation: maximum 100 orders per day
  After probation: limits based on performance metrics
  Seller categories:
    - Individual sellers (small businesses)
    - Brand authorized sellers
    - Brand direct stores (managed by brand itself)

AUTHENTICITY GUARANTEE
  Electronics:
    Only authorized distributor network allowed to sell
    Every unit verified against manufacturer records
    Serial numbers checked on receipt at warehouse (FBP sellers)
  Branded Fashion & Footwear:
    Only brand-authorized sellers permitted
    Random sample checks conducted monthly
  Books and Media:
    Only authorized distributors and publishers
  100% Authenticity Guarantee:
    If you receive a counterfeit item:
    - Full refund issued immediately
    - Rs. 500 store credit compensation
    - Seller account suspended pending investigation
    - Report counterfeit: trust@shopeasy.com

SELLER PERFORMANCE STANDARDS
  Minimum rating: 4.0 out of 5.0
  On-time dispatch rate: minimum 95%
  Order defect rate: maximum 1%
  Return rate: maximum 8%
  Response time to customer queries: within 24 hours
  Sellers falling below standards:
    - Warning issued at first breach
    - Listing suspension at second breach
    - Account suspension at third breach
    - Permanent ban for fraud or counterfeit violations

FULFILMENT OPTIONS

  Seller Self-Fulfilment:
    Seller stores, packs, and ships from their own location
    Seller responsible for packaging quality
    Seller uses our integrated carrier network
    Dispatch SLA: within 2 business days of order

  Fulfilment by ShopEasy (FBS):
    Seller sends inventory to our warehouses
    We handle storage, picking, packing, and shipping
    Benefits for seller:
      - Next-day dispatch guarantee
      - Better search ranking
      - Prime badge eligibility
      - Reduced shipping costs
    Benefits for customer:
      - Guaranteed 24-hour dispatch
      - ShopEasy packaging standards
      - Faster delivery timelines
      - Easier returns (handled by ShopEasy directly)

CUSTOMER PROTECTIONS

  Seller Non-Dispatch:
    If seller does not dispatch within 3 business days:
    - Order automatically cancelled
    - Full refund issued immediately
    - Rs. 50 store credit given to customer as compensation
    - Seller performance metrics penalized

  Wrong Item from Seller:
    Return arranged at seller's expense
    Replacement or full refund within 48 hours
    Customer not responsible for return shipping cost
    Seller bears cost of both return and re-delivery

  Seller Account Issues:
    If seller account is suspended mid-order:
    - ShopEasy fulfils remaining orders from own inventory if possible
    - Full refund issued if fulfilment not possible
    - Customer notified within 24 hours

  Price Gouging Protection:
    Algorithmic price monitoring active 24/7
    Sellers cannot increase prices by more than 20% in 24 hours
    Exception: genuine cost increases with documentation
    Price gouging reports: pricing@shopeasy.com

  Counterfeit Protection:
    All high-value electronics verified before dispatch (FBS)
    Random audits on self-fulfilled electronics sellers
    Customer authenticity reports trigger immediate investigation
    Three verified counterfeit complaints: permanent seller ban

SELLER RATINGS & REVIEWS
  Customers rate sellers separately from products
  Seller rating visible on all their product listings
  Rating criteria:
    - Delivery speed
    - Packaging quality
    - Product accuracy
    - Communication
  Ratings cannot be removed by sellers
  ShopEasy may remove ratings that violate review policy
  Sellers can respond to reviews publicly

PROHIBITED SELLER ACTIVITIES
  Sellers are strictly prohibited from:
    - Listing counterfeit or replica products
    - Listing products without proper licensing
    - Inflating prices during high-demand periods beyond policy
    - Incentivizing customers for positive reviews
    - Creating fake reviews or ratings
    - Communicating with customers outside ShopEasy platform
    - Sharing customer personal data with third parties
    - Listing hazardous items without proper certifications
    - Duplicate listings for same product
  Violation consequences:
    - Minor violations: warning + performance penalty
    - Major violations: immediate listing suspension
    - Fraud or counterfeit: permanent ban + legal action

PROHIBITED PRODUCT CATEGORIES
  The following cannot be sold on ShopEasy:
    - Counterfeit goods of any kind
    - Weapons, firearms, ammunition
    - Illegal drugs or controlled substances
    - Prescription medicines without proper licensing
    - Adult content or obscene material
    - Products that violate intellectual property rights
    - Recalled or unsafe products
    - Stolen goods
    - Wildlife products protected under law
    - Tobacco products without proper licensing

SELLER DISPUTE RESOLUTION
  Sellers can dispute customer claims within 48 hours
  Dispute process:
    Step 1: Seller submits evidence via Seller Central
    Step 2: ShopEasy reviews evidence within 72 hours
    Step 3: Decision communicated to both parties
    Step 4: Final decision by ShopEasy is binding
  Escalation: sellergrievance@shopeasy.com
  Sellers cannot contact customers directly regarding disputes
