
ACCOUNT & MEMBERSHIP
====================

CREATING AN ACCOUNT
  Sign up at shopeasy.com or via the ShopEasy mobile app
  Sign up options:
    - Email address + password
    - Mobile number + OTP
    - Google account (OAuth)
    - Apple ID (OAuth)
    - Facebook account (OAuth)
  OTP verification required for mobile number signup
  Email verification link sent for email signup
  Profile information:
    - Full name
    - Mobile number
    - Email address
    - Date of birth (optional — used for birthday rewards)
    - Gender (optional)
  Save up to 10 delivery addresses
  Save up to 5 payment methods (tokenized — cards never stored raw)

GUEST CHECKOUT
  Order without creating an account
  Enter email and delivery address at checkout
  Order confirmation and tracking sent to email
  Cannot use saved addresses or payment methods
  Cannot earn or redeem ShopCoins reward points
  Returns must be initiated via email link
  Recommend creating account for better experience

PRIME MEMBERSHIP — Rs. 999 per year or Rs. 149 per month

  Delivery Benefits:
    Free unlimited standard delivery on all orders (no minimum)
    Free express delivery on orders above Rs. 499
    Same day delivery at standard delivery price
    Priority dispatch — orders processed first

  Shopping Benefits:
    Early access to all sales — 12 hours before general public
    Exclusive Prime-only deals and flash sales
    Price drop guarantee — if price drops within 24 hours of purchase
    we refund the difference automatically
    Free COD on all orders (normally Rs. 29 per order)
    Free gift wrapping on all orders (normally Rs. 49 per item)

  Return Benefits:
    Extended return window: 45 days (vs 30 days standard)
    Priority return pickup — within 24 hours (vs 48 hours standard)
    Dedicated returns support team

  Rewards Benefits:
    2x ShopCoins on every purchase (vs 1x for standard members)
    500 bonus ShopCoins on Prime signup
    Birthday bonus: 1000 ShopCoins on your birthday

  Support Benefits:
    Priority customer support queue
    Average wait time under 2 minutes
    Dedicated Prime support email: prime@shopeasy.com

  Entertainment Benefits:
    Access to ShopEasy Prime Video (select plans)
    Early access to new product launches

  How to activate Prime:
    My Account > Prime Membership > Subscribe
    Auto-renewal enabled by default
    Cancel anytime — benefits active till end of billing period

  Family sharing:
    Add up to 2 family members to your Prime account
    Family members get all delivery and return benefits
    Family members do not get shopping or rewards benefits
    Manage family sharing: My Account > Prime > Family

SHOPCOINS REWARD POINTS
  Earning ShopCoins:
    Standard members: 1 ShopCoin per Rs. 100 spent
    Prime members: 2 ShopCoins per Rs. 100 spent
    Welcome bonus: 100 ShopCoins on first purchase
    Referral bonus: 500 ShopCoins when referee makes first purchase
    Birthday bonus: 500 ShopCoins (1000 for Prime members)
    Review bonus: 50 ShopCoins for writing a verified product review
    App download bonus: 200 ShopCoins (one time)

  Redeeming ShopCoins:
    1 ShopCoin = Re. 1 discount at checkout
    Apply at checkout under Rewards & Coupons section
    Maximum 20% of order value can be paid via ShopCoins
    Minimum redemption: 100 ShopCoins
    Cannot be combined with certain promotional offers

  ShopCoins validity:
    Expire 1 year after the date they were earned
    Expiry date shown in My Account > ShopCoins
    Reminder email sent 30 days before expiry

  ShopCoins cannot be:
    Transferred to another account
    Converted to cash
    Used to pay delivery charges
    Earned on COD orders (credited after payment confirmed)

ACCOUNT SECURITY
  Two-factor authentication (2FA):
    Enable via My Account > Security > Two-Factor Authentication
    Options: OTP via SMS, OTP via email, authenticator app
  Suspicious login alerts:
    Email and SMS alert for login from new device or location
    Review active sessions: My Account > Security > Active Sessions
    Log out all devices remotely from security settings
  Password requirements:
    Minimum 8 characters
    Must include uppercase, lowercase, number, special character
  Password reset:
    Click Forgot Password on login page
    Reset via email OTP or SMS OTP
    Lost access to both: contact support with government ID proof
  Account freeze:
    Request account freeze if you suspect unauthorized access
    Contact support immediately: security@shopeasy.com

WISHLIST
  Save items for later (unlimited items)
  Price drop alerts: notification when wishlisted item price drops
  Stock alerts: notification when out-of-stock item is available
  Share wishlist via link (useful for gifting)
  Move item from wishlist to cart in one click
  Items saved in wishlist do not reserve stock

NOTIFICATION PREFERENCES
  Email notifications:
    Order updates, shipping confirmations, delivery alerts
    Promotional emails, new arrivals, personalized recommendations
    Manage: My Account > Notifications > Email
  SMS notifications:
    Order updates and OTPs only
    Manage: My Account > Notifications > SMS
  WhatsApp notifications:
    Order updates and delivery alerts (opt-in required)
    Manage: My Account > Notifications > WhatsApp
  Push notifications (app):
    Real-time order tracking, flash sale alerts, price drops
    Enable in app settings or device notification settings

ACCOUNT DELETION
  Request account deletion: My Account > Settings > Delete Account
  All personal data deleted within 30 days
  Order history retained for legal and tax purposes (7 years)
  ShopCoins and store credit forfeited on deletion
  Active orders must be completed or cancelled before deletion
  Prime or Plus membership cancelled and prorated refund issued
